About this site

Nowadays, personal websites and blogs are a bit depreciated, because everybody is posting on facebook. But a personal website is yours, you control all of it from start to end. And so, you can really be proud of it, just like when you craft a small statue with your hands. It’s just unique, it’s you. Over the years, I’ve tested many, many different solutions to build a website: in particular pure html (but it’s really too much work, isn’t it ?) and cms/wordpress (but you’re not in control, it’s heavy, no git, full of vulnerabilities…). »
