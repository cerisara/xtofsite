Personal Page: research

My main research areas are:
  • Automatic speech recognition
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Bayesian models: CRF, HMM, Maximum Entropy, ...
  • Unsupervised training: Dirichlet, HDP, Pitman-Yor priors; Monte-Carle (Gibbs) sampling
  • Interactive learning: Active learning
  • Dependency parsing
  • Semantic role labeling
  • ...
For further information, please have a look at my publications and here.

If you are a researcher and you are interested by one of these domains and would like to collaborate, please contact us: we'll be glad to set-up some form of collaboration if this is relevant to both of us.

Our team proposes some post-doctoral and Ph.D. positions; please contact us if your are interested.
