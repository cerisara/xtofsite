One paper presented at Interspeech 2012
This work investigates possible tradeoffs between transition-based parsers, like the Malt parser, and maximum spanning-tree parsers, like the MSTParser or the MATE parser.
Interspeech is a major international conference about automatic speech processing. Interspeech 2012 took place in Portlang, Oregon, USA.
